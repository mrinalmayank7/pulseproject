Transforming the healthcare with Pulse


Why healthcare

When the pandemic hit, the healthcare system collapsed almost everywhere. Hospitals and healthcare centres were flooded with COVID-19 patients. Families of those who can afford hospital bills were hiring nurses and consulting doctors remotely to keep their loved ones breathing. It seems you can't get treated in hospitals, and now you can't save your loved ones even at home That is when we decided to build Pulse.

A change in the way, you see your health.

Technology can change and improve the productivity of any sector, whether it is finance, real estate, or healthcare. Our ultimate focus is on the growing demand for services in the healthcare sector, rising costs, and the workforce that struggles a lot to meet the demand of millions of people.

Our proposed solution entitled Pulse targets three different areas of the health sector, including specialized diagnosis to assist healthcare experts in clinical decision support and delivering the outcomes in response time with much better accuracy, mental health diagnosis with AI embedded systems, and self-diagnosis via symptoms, provided with medication and more.
